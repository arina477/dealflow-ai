### SourceScrub

**URL:** https://www.sourcescrub.com (product pages 301-redirect to grata.com as of 2026-06-29)
**First seen:** 2026-06-29
**Category overlap with us:** HIGH on sourcing — the de-facto deal-sourcing data layer for the exact lower-middle-market IB/PE segment DealFlow AI targets, but it stops at discovery/list-building: no native outreach execution, no AI buyer-seller matching, no compliance-first workflow.

**Business model:** Annual SaaS (quote-only). Three tiers — Discovery Essentials (~$20–30K/yr), Sourcing Plus (~$30–45K/yr), Winning Professional (~$45–60K/yr), priced per-firm with CRM integration + Data Connect Cloud as add-ons. ~700 firms incl. 35 of top 40 PE firms. **Acquired by Datasite (Aug 8, 2025); Datasite also owns Grata** — product pages now redirect to grata.com; combined entity backed by a $500M CapVest commitment, marketed as end-to-end private-markets intelligence + deal execution. (MARKET_RESEARCH — Datasite press release)

**Key UX patterns worth noting:**
1. **"Never Miss a Deal" sourcing dashboard** — continuous crawl of 290K+ sources vs 17M company profiles; user scoring rules auto-rank inbound targets. Passive discovery, not outreach. (DIRECT_OBSERVATION via WebFetch)
2. **Conference / list synthesis** — upload/connect conference attendee lists; enrich, dedupe, score against thesis. Repeatedly cited as the single strongest differentiated feature ("2 days → 2 hours"). (DIRECT_OBSERVATION)
3. **CRM enrichment as delivery channel** — enriches into Salesforce/DealCloud/Affinity/SugarCRM (priced separately). Positions as a data feed, not a workflow layer. (DIRECT_OBSERVATION)
4. **SourcingGPT** — purpose-built LLM screening/summarizing company profiles vs an investment thesis; "discovery to diligence." No outreach drafting/matching/automation. (DIRECT_OBSERVATION)
5. **Demo-request wall** — no free trial; five-figure floor excludes boutique/solo advisory shops (DealFlow AI's likely pilot segment). (DIRECT_OBSERVATION)

**Pricing structure:** Three tiers (Essentials / Plus / Professional), differentiated by scores/rules/tracked-company counts; ~$20–60K/yr; CRM + API as separate line items; no freemium/trial. (MARKET_RESEARCH)

**Strengths / differentiators:** Source provenance at scale (290K+ sources → 17M profiles); best-in-class conference intelligence; deep founder-owned/bootstrapped coverage (lower-middle-market); nine CRM integrations (switching-cost moat); 35/40 top PE firms; now Datasite-backed ($500M) + Grata's 21M-company coverage.

**Weaknesses / gaps (where DealFlow AI wins):** Zero outreach execution (export to CRM/email separately; reviewers explicitly request email integration); no AI buyer-seller ranked matching (only thesis-scoring of companies); no compliance-first design (no FINRA/SOX framing, no audit logs, no tamper-evident records); pricing excludes boutique advisory; ~1-in-4 G2 reviewers cite data inaccuracy/stale metrics/bounces; steep learning curve + dated UI.
