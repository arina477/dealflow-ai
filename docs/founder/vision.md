# DealFlow AI

## North Star

We're building an internal tool that automates the entire deal sourcing and matching workflow for our M&A advisory firm. Right now we're stitching together five different tools—each with its own login, data format, and compliance headaches. The bet is that a single integrated platform that sources deals, ranks buyer-seller matches with AI, and handles compliant outreach will let us surface and action 10x more qualified matches per week than we do today. That's our lever for scaling advisory revenue without scaling headcount.

## Product one-liner

An AI-powered platform that sources M&A deals, matches buyers and sellers, and executes compliant email outreach in a single workflow.

## Target user

Internal: the founder's M&A advisory firm. Mid-market and lower-middle-market deal sourcing, where manual outreach and fragmented tools are the binding constraint on deal flow volume.

## Core bets

- **Integrated sourcing + AI matching + compliant outreach beats stitched-together tools for M&A workflows**
  - Falsifier: Advisory firms continue to prefer best-of-breed tools even if it means manual integration overhead and compliance friction.

## Differentiation

No integrated platform currently combines deal sourcing, automated buyer-seller matching, and compliance-first email outreach tailored specifically for M&A advisory workflows. Existing solutions force firms to stitch together multiple disconnected tools, each with its own data model and compliance burden.
